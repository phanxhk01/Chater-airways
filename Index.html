A New Kind of Travel

Founded in 2026 and operating exclusively on the GeoFS simulation platform, Chater Airways is Hong Kong’s newest regional virtual airline. Named for Sir Paul Chater, we carry forward his legacy of connection and development with every thoughtful detail.

From our hub at Hong Kong International Airport (VHHH) we serve Kaohsiung (RCKH), Taipei (RCTP), and Xiamen (ZSAM). Our signature product, Chater Stopover, invites travellers to experience Hong Kong for up to seven days at no extra airfare — two cities for the price of one.
